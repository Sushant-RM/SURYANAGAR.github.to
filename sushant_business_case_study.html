Business Case Study: Digital Governance in Smart Cities

Digital governance is transforming how cities operate. By integrating technology into urban planning and management, cities like Barcelona and Singapore are achieving sustainable development goals.

Key highlights include:

  • Barcelona: Citizen platforms enable participatory decision-making.
  • Singapore: Real-time data systems optimize urban resources.
  • Curitiba: Smart waste management ensures resource efficiency.

This case study demonstrates the potential of digital tools to address urban challenges effectively.
